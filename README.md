# credit-risk-analysis-dashboard
Power BI dashboard for credit risk analysis using DAX, Power Query, and interactive visualizations.





📊 Credit Risk Analysis Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

Financial institutions rely on customer financial profiles to assess lending risk and reduce loan defaults. This project analyzes a credit risk dataset using Power BI to identify patterns between customer characteristics and loan repayment behavior.

The dashboard enables decision-makers to understand which customer segments are more likely to default and which factors have the strongest impact on credit risk.

🎯 Business Problem

Banks and lending companies face significant financial losses when borrowers fail to repay their loans.

The goal of this dashboard is to answer questions such as:

Which customers are most likely to default?
Does income affect repayment behavior?
Which loan purposes carry the highest risk?
How does employment length influence default probability?
Which loan grades should receive more attention?

📈 Key Insights
💰 Income
Customers with lower incomes exhibit the highest default rates.
Default rates gradually decrease as customer income increases.
👤 Age
Customers aged 20–24 represent the largest customer segment.
Older age groups have noticeably lower customer counts.
Default risk is relatively higher among younger borrowers.
💼 Employment Length
Customers with less than 3 years of employment show the highest default rate.
Longer employment history is generally associated with lower credit risk.
🏦 Loan Grade
Default rates increase significantly from Grade A toward Grade G.
High-risk grades require stricter approval criteria.
🎯 Loan Purpose

Higher default rates are observed for:

Debt Consolidation
Medical
Home Improvement

Lower default rates are observed for:

Venture
Education
🏠 Home Ownership

Customers who rent properties tend to have higher default rates compared to homeowners.

💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations can help reduce credit risk:

✅ Strengthen approval policies

Apply stricter lending criteria for applicants with:

Low income
Short employment history
High-risk loan grades
✅ Enhance risk-based pricing

Adjust loan interest rates according to customer risk profiles instead of using a fixed pricing strategy.

✅ Improve credit monitoring

Closely monitor customers belonging to high-risk segments after loan approval.

✅ Encourage creditworthy borrowers

Offer preferential loan conditions for customers with:

Stable employment
Higher income
Strong credit grades
✅ Review high-risk loan purposes

Loans requested for Debt Consolidation and Medical expenses should undergo additional verification before approval.
